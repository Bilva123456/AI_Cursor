## 💡 GESTURE COMBINATION TIPS

1. **Be Deliberate**: Make clear distinct gestures, not quick wiggles
2. **Pause Between**: Brief pause (0.5s) between each gesture helps recognition
3. **Use Both Hands**: One hand for gestures, other can rest
4. **Face Camera**: Hand should be visible to camera for all gestures
5. **Good Lighting**: Bright lighting improves recognition accuracy
6. **Consistent Style**: Develop your own consistent gesture style
