Settings Information

Visual Effects: These settings control what visual effect is applied to your cursor when using the gesture control application.

Shape Detection: When enabled, the app will attempt to recognize geometric shapes you draw with your hand.

Gesture Recording: When enabled, you can record custom hand gestures and assign them to specific computer commands.

Sensitivity: Adjust how quickly the app responds to your hand movements. Higher values make the system more responsive but may cause false detections.
