## 💡 TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS

1. **Good Lighting** ☀️
   - Face a light source
   - Avoid shadows on hands
   - Bright room = better detection

2. **Clear Distance** 📏
   - 1-2 feet from camera
   - Hand fully visible
   - Not too close, not too far

3. **Distinct Gestures** ✋
   - Make clear hand shapes
   - Not quick wiggles
   - Pause between gestures

4. **Face Camera** 📹
   - Sit facing your webcam
   - Hand visible at all times
   - Good camera angle

## 🆘 QUICK TROUBLESHOOTING

### Hand Not Detected
- ❌ Too dark? → Turn on lights
- ❌ Too close? → Move back 1-2 feet
- ❌ Camera off? → Check if webcam works

### Commands Not Working
- ❌ Made wrong gesture? → Try again (3+ gestures)
- ❌ No AI Mode? → Press **A** first
- ❌ App needs an app? → Chrome not installed = command fails

### Visual Effects Not Showing
- ❌ Pressed wrong key? → Press **1-6** again
- ❌ Hand not detected? → Move closer to camera
- ❌ No hand visible? → Raise hand to camera
